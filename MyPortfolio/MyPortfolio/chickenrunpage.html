Chicken Run

  1. Home
  2. Chicken Run

Project informations

  • For course: game prototyping
  • Teammates: Philippe Caron - Bouchard
  • Project date: January 8 2025 - Febuary 18 2025
  • Project URL: https://larebelleuse55.itch.io/chickenrun

Catch as many chickens as you can before the timer runs out.

I was in charge of the AI for the chickens. I used the EQS system to make them roam freely and flee from the player when detected through the AIPerception system. I implemented these behaviors using a Behavior Tree. I also handled the player interaction: when a chicken is touched by the player, a point is added to the player's score in the PlayerState, and the score is then displayed on screen.
